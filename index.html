Fisher Creative ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Fish Logger mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information Collection and Use

To provide and improve our Service, we collect several different types of information:

  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect location data to enable mapping features and mark fishing locations. This data is stored securely and is only used to provide you with location-based services within the app.
  • User Content: Information you provide such as fishing logs, photos, and catch details are stored to provide the service.
  • Device Information: Basic device information may be collected for app functionality and improvement.

Google Maps

This app uses Google Maps API to provide mapping features. By using our app, you agree to be bound by Google's Privacy Policy. We only use this service to provide mapping functionality within the app.

Data Storage

We use Firebase, a Google service, to store your data securely. Your data is protected according to Google's security standards and our own security measures.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as necessary to provide the app's services or as required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of location tracking

Account Deletion

To delete your Fish Logger account and all associated data:

  1. Open the Fish Logger app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap on "Account"
  4. Select "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion when prompted
